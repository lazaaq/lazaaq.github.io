WHO I AM

Developer by day, data explorer by night. Here's the full story.

I'm Lana Saiful Aqil, a Fullstack Web Developer and Junior Data Scientist who loves building things that live on the web — from polished frontends to solid backends and everything in between.

My work sits at the intersection of software engineering and data science. I enjoy crafting intuitive user experiences as much as I enjoy diving into datasets to find patterns that drive better decisions.

I started my journey with web development, learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch. Over time, I expanded into React, Node.js, and Python, eventually discovering a passion for data — preprocessing, analysis, visualization, and machine learning.

I'm always learning, always building, and always looking for the next challenge that pushes me to grow as a developer and data practitioner. If you have a project that needs both engineering and data expertise, I'd love to hear about it.
