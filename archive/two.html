About me

The person behind
the keyboard

I write code that's as clean as the products I help ship.

I'm Alex Morgan, a full-stack developer based in San Francisco. I've spent the last 6 years helping startups and scale-ups build products people genuinely enjoy — from zero-to-one launches to hardening existing codebases.

My sweet spot lives at the intersection of engineering rigor and thoughtful UX. I care about readable code, sensible abstractions, and shipping things that work — not just in demos, but in production.

Outside of work, I contribute to open-source tools, write about web performance, and occasionally mentor developers early in their careers.
