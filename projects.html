Alhijaz Tegal (Platform Haji)

Haji AlHijaz is an all-inclusive online platform that simplifies the entire Hajj journey for Muslims worldwide. It offers a user-friendly interface where pilgrims can access essential information, book travel arrangements, secure necessary permits, and receive personalized guidance from a dedicated team of experts. With innovative features like virtual reality tours and interactive maps, Haji AlHijaz enhances the spiritual experience by providing a deeper connection to the holy sites of Makkah and Madinah. This comprehensive platform seamlessly integrates technology and tradition, empowering pilgrims to embark on their Hajj pilgrimage with confidence, convenience, and a profound sense of fulfillment.

Teramina (Shrimp Feed Calculator)

Teramina's Shrimp Feed Calculator is a powerful and intuitive online tool designed to optimize shrimp feeding practices. With a deep understanding of the nutritional requirements of shrimp, this innovative platform allows shrimp farmers to precisely calculate the ideal feed quantities based on the specific size, weight, and growth stage of their shrimp population. By inputting relevant data and parameters, farmers can easily determine the exact amount of feed required, minimizing waste and maximizing growth potential. Teramina's Shrimp Feed Calculator revolutionizes the way shrimp farmers manage their feeding protocols, ensuring efficient resource utilization and promoting sustainable aquaculture practices for a thriving shrimp farming industry.

Stack : HTML CSS Javascript VueJS

School Alumni Database

The website for the school alumni database that I created is the website for the MAN Insan Cendekia Pekalongan school. In it there is data on the alumni of the MAN Insan Cendekia Pekalongan School students, starting from their full names, majors, class names, contacts, profile photos, and so on. The purpose of creating this website is to make it easier for the underclassmen of MAN Insan Cendekia Pekalongan to contact their seniors who are already in college, so they can discuss the continuation of the underclassmen's studies. Because most underclassmen in high school are still confused about their future career choices. With this website, hopefully it can help younger classmates to communicate with alumni who are already in college. The website itself is online and can be used by the general public.
