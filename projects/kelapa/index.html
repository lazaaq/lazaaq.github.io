Who are we?
Kelapa Indonesia which was founded in 2020 is a company that focuses on managing several coconut derived products and at the same time being the main supplier for coconut raw materials and coconut product. We have a strategy for success which is to develop a network of farmers across the coconut plantations that we manage. And we ensure readiness in our processing plants, to be able to produce the best products with international quality. Currently our company has tested with more than 200 coconut farmers in the middle part of Indonesia namely, North Sulawesi.
Vision
Introducing Indonesia's natural resource wealth to the outside world while prospering Indonesian farmers, especially coconut farmers
Mission
Serving coconut products with the best quality Working with local farmers to improve their quality of life Prioritizing client satisfaction
